Elite Speeding & Technical Camp

Our Elite series camps are geared towards athletes looking to take their game to the next level. With specific training on technical skills, speed, agility and strength, we offer a glimpse into what that next level looks like. The athletes will push to fine tune their skills while being asked to work individually and in small groups.

 

At Academy 38 camps we pride ourselves on a professional staff to help empower these young athletes and encourage them to jump out of their comfort zones and know that it’s ok to DREAM. BELIEVE. INSPIRE.
