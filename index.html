What is Academy38?

Academy38 is an Elite program that encourages young athletes to be confident and push themselves to be their best on and off the field. With high level training on the field, we also strive to find the moments to teach about the importance of team, healthy lifestyle and believing in ourselves. At ACADEMY38 our motto is DREAM. BELIEVE. INSPIRE because we believe in the beauty of having a dream, believing in the dream and inspiring others.
