Summer Soccer Camp

These camps offer much more than just an on-field experience. We have teamed up with some incredible woman who have experiences at the top club, collegiate and professional level as players and coaches. We offer athletes sessions on technical & tactical awareness, discuss positive leadership roles, and a special insight on sports nutrition. During camp, we will also explore goal setting and how to make all the of the above components more attainable in the athlete’s eye.

 

At Academy 38 camps we pride ourselves on a professional staff to help empower these young athletes and encourage them to jump out of their comfort zones and know that it’s ok to DREAM. BELIEVE. INSPIRE.

 
  • Girls ages 9-18
  • Speed & agility, injury prevention, technical skills, leadership, team building, goal setting & sports nutrition
